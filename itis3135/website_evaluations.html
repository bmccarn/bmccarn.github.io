Website Evaluation

Apple

This is the website of the tech giant, Apple.

  • Contrast:Apple does a great job with contrast on their website. The colors of the font stand out nicely against the background. It is very east to read and and pleasing to look at.
  • Repetition: There is very little repetition when it comes to the colors, layouts and information on the pages. It seems very well thought out and has been implemented well.
  • Alignment: They have done a very good job of centering what needs to be centered and aligning other items where they need to be. Nothing seems awkardly placed and everything seems to be placed with a purpose.
  • Proximity: The content on the pages is spaced well. The pages don't seem to be crowded when looking through the website.


Yale School of Art

This is the website of the Yale School of Art...an absolute eyesore.

  • Contrast: Yale has done an absolutely terrible job with this website. The contrast is somewhat there in parts, but for the most part it is an eyesore. Many of the text blocks blend into the dark background making it very hard to read.
  • Repetition: The repetition on this website is awful. Mainly the black and white text blocks that are seen consistently throughout the homepage. There should be some variety to the content.
  • Alignment: The alignment on the website is possibly the worst thing about it. The title of the homepage isn't centered and everything seems to be left justified.
  • Proximity: The proximity of the material is very bad. The blocks of content on the homepage are randomly spaced from each other. This makes it very hard to follow along and make sense of what the links/content is for.

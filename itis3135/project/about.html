Hello and Welcome!

To begin with, thank your for visiting my page. I'm so glad you're here! Whether you're looking for a wedding photographer, senior pictures, or simply looking for some prints to hang on your wall, you've come to the right place.

Here at Captured Moments we understand how much those special events mean to you. We take pride in delivering high quality images that you can look back on years from now. Don't let these special moments slip away!

Photography has always held a special place in my heart. The ability to freeze time and capture a memory is something that is so special and unique. I used to take pictures trying to stop time. I felt frantic to clutch past recollections, instead of living in the now. In the long run I understood that I need to take pictures not to look back, but rather with an end goal to appreciate the present minute.

When I glance back at old photos, I would prefer not to feel that mixed sentimentality for what used to be.

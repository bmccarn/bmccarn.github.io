Project Design


Project Overview:

  • For this project I will be designing a website for my client to promote and display their photography business.
  • The intended users of the website will be individuals who might hire my client to do photography shoots for them.
  • The website will contain contact information for me client, a gallery of photos they have take, as well as prices and packages offered.

Site Layout:


Home Page Layout:

This page will be the landing page for the website. It will have the navigation bar at the top and possibly a few sample pictures giving the user an idea of what the website is about.


About Us Page Layout:

THis page will be relatively short and simple. It will give the user background information on the business/business owner.


Contact Us Page Layout:

This page will provide the user with contact information to contact the business owner (phone number, email, etc.) and schedule a photo shoot.


Photo Gallery Page Layout:

This page will have some sort of slideshow that displays photos the business owner has taken to give them a better idea about the product.


Prices and Packages Page Layout:

This page will have a list of the prices of the packages the business owner offers. There will be some sort of calculator that the user can use to add up picture print purchases and/or photoshoot packages in order to get an idea of the total price.


Shopping Cart Page Layout:

This page will allow the user to see a breakdown of what is in their shopping care along with the prices including tax.
